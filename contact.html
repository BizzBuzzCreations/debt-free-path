Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions our team hears most often when people first get in touch.

Will contacting you affect my credit score?

No. Simply reaching out for advice will not affect your credit score in any way. A credit search will only occur if and when you choose to proceed with a specific formal debt solution through one of our regulated partners.

Is your advice really free?

Yes. Your initial consultation and debt assessment are completely free of charge with no obligation. We will always tell you clearly and upfront if any charges apply to a specific solution before you decide anything.

Am I eligible for an IVA or DMP?

Eligibility depends on your individual circumstances — including your income, debt level, and the type of debts you hold. Our advisors will assess your full situation and recommend the most suitable option for you. There's no obligation to proceed with anything.

How quickly can I get help?

Once you submit your enquiry, a specialist will typically be in touch within 24 hours on business days. If you need help urgently, please call us directly on +44 7446461601 and we'll do our best to assist you straight away.

What areas of the UK do you cover?

We help clients across England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland. Some debt solutions — such as Protected Trust Deed and Sequestration — are specific to Scotland and governed by Scottish law. Our advisers will guide you to the right option for your location.

Important Information Debt Free Path provides debt solution referral and advisory services to UK residents. We are committed to following all guidelines set out by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and relevant UK legislation. An IVA is a formal insolvency solution — it may affect your credit rating and is recorded on the Insolvency Register. A DMP is an informal solution and your creditors are not legally bound by it. We recommend you carefully consider all options before proceeding with any debt solution.

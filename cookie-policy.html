Cookie Policy

How Debt Free Path uses cookies and similar technologies — and how you can manage your preferences at any time.

1 Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Debt Free Path ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. It outlines what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control their use.

2 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you browse a website. They are widely used to ensure websites function efficiently and to collect information about how visitors interact with the site.

3 Types of Cookies We Use

We may use the following categories of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies — These cookies are essential for the website to operate correctly and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Analytics / Performance Cookies — These help us understand how visitors engage with our website by collecting anonymous information such as page visits and traffic sources.
  • Functionality Cookies — These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as preferences) to provide a more personalised experience.

4 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly and securely
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Analyse visitor behaviour to enhance our content and services

5 Third-Party Cookies

We may allow selected third-party services (such as analytics providers) to place cookies on your device. These cookies help us measure and improve the effectiveness of our website. These third parties are responsible for their own cookie practices.

6 Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites

Please note that restricting cookies may impact the functionality of certain parts of our website.

7 Your Consent

When you first visit our website, you may be presented with a cookie notice or banner. By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies as outlined in this policy, unless you choose to disable them.

8 Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements or how we use cookies. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
